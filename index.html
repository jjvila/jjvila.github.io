About Me

I am a graphic designer, photographer, and computer programmer with a focus on developing UI and other front-end systems.

Complimenting my professional work is a B.S. in computer science from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

UI & Programming

Designing a great user interface involves hours of planning and work, but the process is energizing and suspensful.

The best part of the process is that the UI/UX field is always evolving with new ideas and technolgies.

Graphic Design

Graphic design is an indispensable talent for which I am grateful to have. I have designed flyers, maps, blueprints, and even UI mockups.

The human heart depicted above became a featured picture at Wikipedia, where I first learned about vector art.

Photography

Photography is the hobby I escape to when the sunset is beautiful, or the plant in my yard blooms its first flower.

My focus is nature, but over the years I have done family portraits, architecture, night, and stock photography.
